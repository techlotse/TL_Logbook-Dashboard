Operational Disclaimer

TL-Logbook-Dashboard is a personal analytics and visualization tool. It is not certified, audited, approved, or intended for operational flight planning, flight safety decisions, licensing, recency, currency, training records, legal record keeping, maintenance decisions, insurance submissions, or regulatory compliance.

Always verify totals, routes, PIC, dual, XC, and PIC XC values against your official logbook and applicable aviation rules before relying on them.

Privacy Notice

Uploaded logbook PDFs may contain personal data, flight history, aircraft registrations, instructor names, airport usage, and other sensitive aviation information. The application stores uploaded PDFs and generated summaries only inside a server-side browser session directory for the configured session lifetime.

  • Default retention: 24 hours, controlled by SESSION_TTL_SECONDS.
  • Users can clear their active session from the dashboard.
  • No account system, analytics tracking, advertising tracking, or marketing cookies are included by default.
  • Logbook data is processed to display dashboard metrics, charts, tables, airports, and routes.

If you host this service publicly, configure transport security, access controls, backups, logs, and retention to match your own data-protection obligations.

Cookies

The application uses one necessary HTTP-only session cookie to keep each browser session isolated from other active users. This cookie is required for the service to work and is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

External Services

The dashboard uses Leaflet for maps and displays map tiles from external tile providers. Depending on deployment configuration, those providers may receive technical request data such as IP address, user agent, requested tiles, and timestamps. For stricter privacy requirements, self-host third-party assets and use a compliant tile provider or self-hosted tile server.
